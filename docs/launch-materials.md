## Indie Hackers Post

**Title:** I Built an E-commerce AI Toolkit in 1 Day - Here's What I Learned

**Body:**

### The Problem

Running an e-commerce store means drowning in repetitive tasks. Every day:
- Check if competitors lowered prices
- Write social posts for the same products
- Calculate if your marketing spend is actually working

I was tired of this. So I built a solution.

### The Solution

E-commerce Skills Bundle - three AI skills that work together:

1. **Competitive Monitor** ($29/mo)
Track rival pricing, products, and reviews automatically.

2. **Social Poster** (Free)
Generate a week's worth of social content in minutes.

3. **Analytics Dashboard** (Free)
Calculate CAC, LTV, AOV, conversion rates instantly.

### Built With

- OpenClaw (automation framework)
- Claude Code (AI coding)
- MiniMax M2.7 (inference)

### What I Learned

1. **Start with free, monetize later** - The free skills build trust, paid features generate revenue
2. **Natural language > complex UIs** - Just say what you want
3. **Vertical focus wins** - E-commerce is huge, but specific

### What's Next

- More platform integrations (Shopify API, Amazon SP-API)
- Automated alerting (Discord, Slack, email)
- Price optimization suggestions
